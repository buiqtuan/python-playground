Install numpy and scipy manually:

Download numpy and scipy from this link: https://www.lfd.uci.edu/~gohlke/pythonlibs/#numpy

- Numpy: numpy-1.15.1+mkl-cp37-cp37m-win32.whl 

- Scipy: scipy-1.1.0-cp37-cp37m-win32.whl

If these packages were installed before, remove it before reinstall (pip uninstall <package-name>)
